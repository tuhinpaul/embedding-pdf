This document demonstrates how to embed pdf files in HTML (and PHP/ASP.Net/Java EE/ Ruby on Rails/Node.js, etc.).
Note: I am addressing modern browsers (as of May 06, 2017); and not focusing on the strange behaviours (if any) of IE.
I am showing four options here. You may adjust width and height according to your needs.

Option 1/4: <object> tag

Note: I did not find a standard fullscreen option.
More on object: embed on MDN

Source Code:

<object
	data="test.pdf"
	type="application/pdf"
	width="400"
	height="600">
	<span style='color: red;'>If the old browser does not support <object> tag, this red line will appear: <a href="test.pdf">test.pdf</a></span>
</object>

Option 2/4: <embed> tag

Note: I did not find a standard fullscreen option.
Warning: <embed> does not have a closing tag; and you should use HTML5.
More on embed: embed on MDN

Source Code:

<embed
	src="test.pdf"
	type="application/pdf"
	width="400"
	height="600">

Option 3/4: <iframe> tag

Note: In HTML v 4.01, <iframe> has a fullscreen option. Check MDN link below.
More on iframe: iframe on MDN

Source Code:

<iframe
	src="test.pdf"
	width="400"
	height="600"></iframe>
